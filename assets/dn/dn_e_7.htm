7 Jaliya Sutta: About Jaliya

[159] 1. THUS HAVE I HEARD.178 Once the Lord was staying at Kosambi, in the Ghosita Park. And two wanderers, Mandissa and Jaliya, the pupil of the wooden-bowl ascetic, came to him, exchanged courtesies with him and sat down to one side... (verses 1-5 = Sutta 6, verses 15-19). [160]

Thus the Lord spoke, and the two wanderers rejoiced at his words.
